Environmentally Friendly Questionnaire

We have ten questions in total. "Always" worths 10 points, "Sometimes" worths 5 points, and "Never" worths 0 points. The user needs to answer if they always, sometimes, or never do these environmentally friendly actions. After answering ten questions, the user will receive a comment based on their total score.
